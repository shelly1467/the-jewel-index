Privacy Policy

The Jewel Index is a jewelry guide created to share style inspiration, jewelry education, and curated browsing ideas. This page explains how information may be handled when visitors use this website.

Current Policy

How Information Is Handled

The Jewel Index does not directly sell products or process purchases on this website. If visitors click a retailer or affiliate link in the future, they may be taken to a third-party website. Those websites have their own privacy policies, terms, prices, shipping information, and return policies.

Information We May Collect

The Jewel Index may collect basic website information such as pages visited, browser type, device type, and general usage activity if analytics tools are added. This information helps improve the website experience.

Affiliate and Third-Party Links

Some pages may include affiliate links in the future. If affiliate links are added, The Jewel Index may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. When you click a third-party retailer link, you are leaving The Jewel Index and should review that retailer’s privacy policy and purchase terms.

Cookies and Analytics

This website may use cookies or similar tools in the future to understand website traffic and improve content. Visitors can manage cookies through their browser settings.

Personal Information

The Jewel Index does not currently collect payment information or process orders. If a contact form, email signup, or newsletter is added later, this policy may be updated to explain what information is collected and how it is used.

Children’s Privacy

The Jewel Index is intended for a general audience and is not directed toward children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the website grows, especially if affiliate links, analytics, email signups, or new tools are added.

Questions

This Privacy Policy may be updated as The Jewel Index grows. If a contact page or email address is added later, visitors will be able to use it for privacy-related questions.
