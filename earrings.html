What To Notice

Earring Details to Compare

  • Length, width, and drop measurements to understand scale beyond close-up imagery.
  • Backing type, closure style, and how secure the fastening appears for the intended use.
  • Metal tone, stone placement, and how much visual movement the pair may create.
  • Retailer notes covering materials, care details, shipping expectations, and return policies.
